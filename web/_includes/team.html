Equipo organizativo

El Community Leadership Summit original fue concebido en 2009 por Jono Bacon, actualmente Senior Director of Community en XPRIZE, anteriormente community manager para la comunidad global de Ubuntu, y autor del libro The Art of Community de O’Reilly. Lo ha organizado desde entonces cada año. El encuentro es el primer paso para ayudar a gestores y líderes de comunidades a definir y refinar su trabajo, compartir conocimiento y hacer conexiones de forma neutral.

El CLSxMadrid está organizado por un equipo que incluye a Manrique López (involucrado en diversas comunidades Open Source y en el Community Leadership Summit original), María Sánchez (que ha trabajado para diversas comunidades y empresas Open Source en tareas de comunicación y marketing), César García (activo las comunidades en IoT y MakespaceMadrid), Andrés L. Martínez (que trabaja en el Google Developer Program), Laura Vignali (co-organizadora del Codemotion España) y otros.

Organization Team

The original Community Leadership Summit was conceived back in 2009 by Jono Bacon, who is currently the Senior Director of Community at XPRIZE, former community manager for the global Ubuntu community, and author of The Art of Community by O’Reilly. He has organized it each year since then. He put together the summit as a first step in helping community managers and leaders to define and refine their work, share knowledge and make connections in a vendor neutral way.

The CLSxMadrid is lead by a team of organizers including Manrique López (active in several Open Source communities and involved on the original Community Leadership Summit), María Sánchez (working for several Open Source communities on communication and marketing activities), César García (active in IoT and MakespaceMadrid communities), Andrés L. Martínez (working at Google Developer Program), Laura Vignali (Codemotion Spain co-organizer) and others.
